Game Development

Light of Torifune Shrine

Light of Torifune Shrine is my first serious and fully complete project. It is a one-shot side-scrolling game where you must illuminate obstacles.
Developed in Godot 4 in both languages C# and GDScript, the project takes advantage of the engine's dynamic lighting system, normal maps, and specular maps to create its visual style.
